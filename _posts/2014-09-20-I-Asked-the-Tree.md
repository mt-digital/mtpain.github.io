I wasn't sure
</p>
<p class="prose">

If I should drill into the tree 
</p>
<!-- ![I looked left](/resources/left_hook.jpg) -->

To hang the clothesline,
</p>
<p class="prose">
to use less energy
</p>

<p class="prose">
my dead brothers

yes!
